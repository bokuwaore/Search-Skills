**Common Vulnerabilities and Exposure (CVE)**
universal dictionary of known vunerabilities (CVE-YEAR-NUMBER) vunerabilities are scored from impact, complexity, and availability. (ExploitDB is one example)
- Impact: What damage can this vunerability lead to
- Complexity: Easy to exploit or not?
- Availability: how likely is it that someone can explit this
